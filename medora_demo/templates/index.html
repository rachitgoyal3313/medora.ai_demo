Frequently Asked Questions

How does voice-to-text prescription work? +
Our AI-powered system uses advanced speech recognition technology to convert doctor's spoken prescriptions into structured digital format. It understands medical terminology in 22+ Indian languages and automatically formats the prescription with proper dosages, instructions, and drug interactions.
Is patient data secure and compliant? +
Yes, we follow strict HIPAA compliance and Indian data protection regulations. All patient data is encrypted end-to-end, stored securely, and access is controlled through multi-factor authentication. We never share patient information without explicit consent.
How does the system integrate with existing hospital software? +
Our platform offers seamless integration with popular EMR systems through APIs. We support HL7 FHIR standards and can customize integration based on your hospital's existing infrastructure. Our technical team provides full support during the integration process.
What languages are supported for voice commands? +
We support 22+ Indian languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Bengali, Marathi, Gujarati, Kannada, Malayalam, Punjabi, Urdu, and many regional dialects. Our AI continuously learns and improves recognition accuracy for medical terminology in each language.
